What is camping?

Camping is a broad term but in its essence, camping is a way of getting away from the hassle of urban life, to a more natural environment for a limited time.

Why you should try camping?

There are many benefits of camping.It can reduce your stress and also helps you to unplug from your screens and embrace outdoors. It helps to strengthen bonds between people that camp together. You also develop new skills as a person like setting up tents, tying knots, starting a fire. Most importantly, it increases your survival skills, getting you a higher chance to win the hunger games if needed.
